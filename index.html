First ever large-scale RvR

Castle Siege.

This game was the first to introduce World Siege,

RvR content where guilds engage in large scale battles for control over castles.

Castle Siege,

where players enjoy the process of achieving a common goal by cooperating and overcoming opposition,

became the hallmark of Korean online MMORPGs.

Leading the growth and development of Korean online games,

this game introduced new authentic systems and content,

including guilds led by a monarch,

a large scale battle called Castle Siege and the Weapon Reinforcement System,

which became the standard for numerous later MMORPGs.
